How can I start
selling posters?

  • 01.Design your posters

    Don't be put off if you have minimal design experience. Take inspiration from other sellers and begin designing posters on Canva or Photoshop.

  • 02.List your products on Etsy

    It's very easy to begin selling on Etsy. Simply upload your products and wait for the sales to roll in.

  • 03.Let PrintShrimp handle the shipping

    Simply upload your orders through our easy to use order portal. We will print and ship your orders, and then provided you with an invoice and tracking.
